My Story

Hello, I'm Chethan Kumar

I am a passionate web developer based in Mysore, India, with expertise in creating responsive and visually appealing websites. My journey in web development began during my college years when I discovered my passion for turning ideas into functional, beautiful digital experiences.

With a focus on clean, efficient code and user-centered design, I approach each project with creativity and attention to detail. I specialize in front-end development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm constantly expanding my skills to include the latest web technologies.

Beyond coding, I'm an avid learner who enjoys staying updated with emerging trends in web development. I believe that the best websites are those that seamlessly blend aesthetics with functionality, creating memorable experiences for users.
